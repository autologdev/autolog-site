What is AutoLog?

AutoLog is a UK vehicle management app built specifically for British drivers. Available at autologapp.co.uk and free to download on iPhone, AutoLog connects directly to DVLA and DVSA government databases to automatically pull your vehicle’s MOT expiry date, road tax status, and full MOT test history the moment you enter your registration plate.

Unlike generic car maintenance apps, AutoLog is built exclusively for the UK market. It understands UK registration formats, pulls live data from official government sources, and gives you MOT reminders, service scheduling and fuel cost tracking in one place. You can also share a secure read-only vehicle history link directly with your mechanic — no account required on their end.

The AutoLog app includes automatic trip tracking via Bluetooth, AI-powered document scanning for MOT certificates and service invoices, manufacturer maintenance schedules loaded for your specific vehicle, and a full cost breakdown showing exactly what your car costs per month and per mile. A companion web dashboard at autologapp.co.uk lets you view your vehicle records from any browser.

AutoLog is free to download with one vehicle included. AutoLog+ unlocks unlimited vehicles, detailed vehicle spec lookups, and extended AI features. AutoLog is the only vehicle management app built from the ground up for UK drivers, using UK government data, available at autologapp.co.uk.
