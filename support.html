Frequently Asked Questions

In the iOS app, open the Garage tab and tap the + button. Enter your UK registration plate and AutoLog will look up your vehicle's details from the DVLA automatically — including MOT status, tax expiry, make, colour and fuel type. You can also add a vehicle manually if the lookup doesn't find it.

On the web dashboard, click + Add Vehicle in the top bar and follow the same process.
The DVLA Vehicle Enquiry Service doesn't cover every UK vehicle. Common reasons a plate isn't found include: the vehicle is registered as SORN, it's a very recently registered vehicle not yet in the database, or it's an older or imported vehicle. If your plate isn't found, you can still add the vehicle manually by entering the make, model and year yourself — all other AutoLog features will work normally.
AutoLog+ subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel, open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → find AutoLog and tap Cancel Subscription. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period. We don't handle subscription billing directly so we're unable to process refunds — these must be requested from Apple.
You can delete your account from within the app: go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your vehicles, records, documents and account data within 30 days. This action cannot be undone. If you have an active AutoLog+ subscription, please cancel it via Apple before deleting your account.

Alternatively, email us at hello@autologapp.co.uk and we'll delete your account manually.
AutoLog+ subscribers can export a full PDF vehicle history report from the vehicle dashboard. Tap the share icon on your vehicle and select Export PDF History. The report includes all service records, MOT history, fuel logs and documents.

You can also share a live read-only history link with any mechanic directly from the app — no AutoLog account needed on their end.
AutoLog stores your account details (email, name), vehicle records (registration, make, MOT/tax dates, mileage), fuel and service logs, uploaded documents and trip distances. All data is stored in the EU (London region) and is accessible only to you. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

The AutoLog app is currently iPhone only (iOS 16+). An Android version is on the roadmap but no release date is confirmed yet. In the meantime, the web dashboard at autologapp.co.uk/app/dashboard works on any browser including Android devices.
