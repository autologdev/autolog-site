2. Description of Service

AutoLog is a vehicle management application for UK residents. It allows users to track MOT and tax status, store service history, log fuel costs, scan vehicle-related documents, monitor maintenance schedules, and share vehicle summaries with mechanics.

AutoLog connects to official UK government data sources (DVLA and DVSA) to provide accurate, up-to-date vehicle information. The service is provided via an iOS application distributed through the Apple App Store.
